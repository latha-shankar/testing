Bridal Makeup

Bridal makeup is done for a minimum of three hours by using high quality and longlasting cosmetic products to improve the look and feel of the bride. Along with the makeup, suitable jewellery and costume are also worn by the bride which makes the look perfect and complete.



Saree Prepleating

Pre-pleated sarees are a contemporary version of the conventional Indian sari. With pre-pleats, these ready sarees are quite easy to wear convenient to move around in. In most of these designer ensembles, the pallu is also a statement in itself- a gathered extension with ornamental add-ons.It is ideal to create 6-7 pleats measuring about 5-6 inches wide. The pleats should be even in width and should go straight one over the other neatly. Tuck the pleats facing towards your left and slightly towards the left side of the navel.
